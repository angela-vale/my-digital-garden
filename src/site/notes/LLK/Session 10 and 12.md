I have always thought Thallen a good, noble knight, but I must remember that they may be a knight, but they are a soldier, too. And that means they can do some unsavoury things.

We have released Onyx, our prisoner of the [[Stars Eternal\|Stars Eternal]], after learning what we could from him thanks to Capella Charming him. I worry what he will say to his people, if he finds them. They put a spell on me to force me to reveal secrets, when my wounds became infected, they forced me to drink a healing potion, they blinded me by removing my mask, they left me in the middle of nowhere, they cut off my thumb. 

I am still surprised by that. Not that Fen did it. No, that would be something one might expect from a bounty hunter. No, I am surprised that Thallen allowed it. 

I did not know them well before this adventure, but their reputation has always been sterling. What I have seen from them since is well in line with that reputation. But this…? To condone torture?

Was it revenge for the assassination attempt on Urion? For the danger to Capella? Or, was it for some other reason? I will need to make sure I stay on their good side and watch. 

No matter, we released Onyx. I left him my knife and had Ref chew through his binds once we were far enough away. If he manages to survive and find his way back to his people, he will have quite a tale to tell. 

We arrive at Lake Kinkora not long before Ibal rose. There was a campfire there, and an old woman heavily bent with age there to meet us. Elin knows her. I…I had never thought this a possibility, that I would meet someone the voice knew, that there would be proof that it had a real connection to the world. 

Her name is Ilsevel of Shulvani, and Kigal sent her to us to assist us. The buildings here are full of supplies, left by previous travellers so that they could be taken by those in need. She told us to take what we need and her tribe will replace it for us. We took all that we needed. 

More than her, Kigal also sent Berry Boy! I remember when Maywin visited Rovanna and brought Berry Boy. I snuck down to the stables where he was housed and saw him up close. Huge and fluffy and not nearly as vicious as one would expect from a dire owlbear. There was something intelligent in his eyes. 

Ard took to him at once, which makes me believe that Berry Boy is, in fact, another fay creature. 

Islevel has made a saddle for us so that we may ride Berry Boy to the bridge, and maybe even all the way to Attan. 

But, she also brought news. Monsters have risen up in Attan, forcing the people who live there to flee. In the south, [[LLK/Fort Calagal\|Fort Calagal]] is facing an onslaught of monsters, and north, [[Fort Sitern\|Fort Sitern]] is also under attack. No word from Rovanna since King Urion’s death. It seems that everywhere, there is chaos.  I wonder if getting Tau to return will be enough to settle this madness. 

Still, it felt good to rest by a fire, with food prepared by someone else, a nice (if cool) bath in the lake, and a roof overhead, even if it is merely a piece of canvas. 

I must get Islevel alone tomorrow, so I can ask about Elin directly. If she can confirm that Elin is real, and no demon, then I have proof that I am not mad. In fact, if her tribe is at the bridge, maybe Elin is among them. Might I be able to meet her, face to face?  Time will tell…
