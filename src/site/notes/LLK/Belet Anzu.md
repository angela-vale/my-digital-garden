[[LLK/Tau\|Tau]]’s right-hand and sword bearer, he rules in her place when she is touring the mortal realm and otherwise serves as her attendant and messenger. Very rarely leaves the divine realm himself. Carries the Sun’s sword of impossible feats.

Patron of [[LLK/Thallen\|Thallen]]
